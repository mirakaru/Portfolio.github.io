About Me

Hello, and welcome to my corner of the web! I'm Haikel Henia, a dedicated web developer with a passion for embracing new technologies and tackling challenges through coding!

This space serves as my personal hub, where I share insights into the dynamic world of web development that interests me and inspires me.

My academic journey includes pursuing an AEC in Internet of Things and Artificial Intelligence at Collège Ahuntsic, where I delve into courses like Artificial Intelligence, Development of Intelligent Objects, and Database and Megadata Management, Web Development, and Multiplatform Application Development.

Alongside my technical expertise, I hold an MBA in Marketing and Pharmaceutical Management from Université de Laval, Quebec, and a Pharmacy Degree from UMF VICTOR BAEBS Timisoara, Romania. My professional experience spans roles in bilingual customer service, sales, and a key role as a Pharmacist Manager.

Thank you for exploring my website and learning more about my journey. I trust you've enjoyed perusing my blog posts, finding them insightful and engaging. For a regular dose of web development trends and tips, feel free to subscribe to my newsletter. I value your feedback and suggestions, so please don't hesitate to reach out—I'm eager to connect with fellow enthusiasts like you!
